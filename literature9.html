Literature Review 9

Project Name: Data Governance and Compliance for School Analytics

Summary: This research discusses policies, controls, and auditing required for compliant student data analytics, including role-based access and audit trails.

Key Outcomes:

  • RBAC design and data minimization
  • Retention, consent, and audit requirements
  • Incident response and monitoring

Technical Features:

  • IAM, audit logs, encryption, DLP
  • PII masking/tokenization strategies
  • Compliance workflows and reporting
